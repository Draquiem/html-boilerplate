Quick Trading Guide

Simple Strategies You Can Use Now!

Supply and Demand

This is a simple strategy that solely depends on finding point of interest.

Here is a list of things you will need to do to trade with this strategy

  1. Identify where supply and demand zones are, this will give you a good indication of support and resistance
  2. Determine the trend before you transact your trade. This will increase the likelihood of profit. Remember that the trend is your friend.
  3. If it is an uptrend, you will long when price reaches the demand zone if it is a down trend, you will short when price reaches the supply zone.

Moving Average

Trading the moving average is very simple to follow, however, it takes a lot of discretion to make this strategy work.

This is a strategy that can easily be automated, but the reason why it isn't is due to market variance. You can argue that one can build a neural network to combat this issue, and this is true, but what retailer do you know (aside from me) that can build a neural network trading system from their bedroom?

Here is the steps to trading with this strategy.

  1. Pick a few timeframes to trade with, this is an intraday trading strategy; you will not be holding trades long
  2. Once timeframe is determined, assess the trend.
  3. Depending on the trend, you will either long or short whenever price revisits the moving average.
